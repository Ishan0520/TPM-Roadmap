# 📖 Overview

Applied Intuition is a software infrastructure company focused on accelerating the development of **autonomous systems and software-defined vehicles**.

Founded in **2017**, the company provides simulation, validation, and development tools used by automotive manufacturers, robotics companies, and defense organizations.

Rather than building vehicles themselves, Applied Intuition develops the **software platforms that enable other companies to build autonomous vehicles faster and more safely.**

Today the company operates as a **developer infrastructure platform for autonomy**, supporting customers across automotive, trucking, defense, and robotics industries.

# 🚀 Why Applied Intuition

What makes Applied Intuition particularly interesting is its role as the **developer infrastructure layer for autonomous systems.**  

While many companies focus on building autonomous vehicles themselves, Applied Intuition builds the tools that allow those companies to develop, test, and validate autonomy much faster. In many ways, it plays a role similar to cloud infrastructure providers in software development—enabling an entire ecosystem of companies to build complex systems more efficiently.

This position at the center of the autonomy ecosystem creates a unique engineering environment where simulation systems, AI development, and vehicle software must all work together. For someone interested in complex technical systems and large-scale engineering coordination, Applied Intuition represents an opportunity to work on infrastructure that supports the future of autonomous mobility.
